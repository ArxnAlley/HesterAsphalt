Asphalt Services in Leesburg

What we do here.

Leesburg is where Hester Asphalt lives — and has since 2006. Our equipment is parked on Grimsley Road, our crew drives these roads daily, and most of our work in town comes from someone who saw us sealcoating two streets over. From driveways off SR-28 to farm lanes out toward Fairfield Township, we know the local pavement — and the way Highland County clay holds water against it.

That local knowledge shapes every recommendation. We watch where water actually runs, schedule sealcoating around familiar weather and fill cracks before winter temperatures start changing. If something is not right after we leave, we are five minutes away, and we act like it.

Clay soil, real drainage

Highland County clay holds water against pavement bases. Local jobs get extra attention on slope and stone depth because we have seen what happens when that preparation is skipped.
