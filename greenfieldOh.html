What we do here.

Greenfield is genuinely local for us, close enough that the drive to your property can be shorter than the site visit. We sealcoat and repair driveways across town and the surrounding townships, keep business and church lots along Jefferson Street looking sharp, and fill cracks in fall before Paint Creek valley winters reach the pavement.
