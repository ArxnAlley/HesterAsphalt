The Work

From inspection to finished lot.

  1. Phase 1 · Assess

    Read the Surface

    Every plan starts with what's really there: base condition, drainage paths, crack patterns, traffic wear. The site visit decides the scope — not a sales script.

  2. Phase 2 · Restore

    Repair, Fill, Seal

    Failures get cut out and patched, cracks get hot rubberized filler, and the whole surface gets commercial grade sealer — in one sequenced visit wherever possible.

  3. Phase 3 · Finish

    Stripe & Open

    We chalk the layout, stripe stalls and lanes by machine and check the signage. The lot goes back to work on the schedule we promised.
