HESTER ASPHALT SEALCOATING — VIDEO ASSET GENERATION PROMPTS
============================================================

The hero currently uses real company footage (heroLanding.mp4, carried over
from the original shoot). The prompts below are production-ready inputs for
AI video generation (Runway Gen-4 / Sora / Veo) or a shot list for a real
drone videographer, to expand the footage library. Target: 1080p+, 24fps,
8-12 second loops, muted, dark/late-afternoon grading to match the site's
charcoal-and-safety-orange design system.

------------------------------------------------------------
1. HERO ALTERNATE — FRESH MAT STEAM (loop)
------------------------------------------------------------
Low tracking shot, 8 inches above fresh black asphalt, moving slowly forward.
Heat shimmer and thin steam rising from the new mat. A paver machine occupies
the upper third, slightly out of focus. Crew members in hi-vis yellow shirts.
Late afternoon sun flaring low on the horizon, warm orange rim light against
deep charcoal tones. Cinematic, shallow depth of field, seamless loop.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. DRONE FLYOVER — COMMERCIAL LOT REVEAL
------------------------------------------------------------
Top-down drone shot at 200 ft, slowly descending over a freshly sealcoated
commercial parking lot. Jet-black surface with crisp white stall lines and a
yellow fire lane. The camera yaws 15 degrees during descent. Surrounding
green Ohio farmland and a brick commercial building edge the frame.
Golden-hour shadows stretch across the lot. Smooth gimbal motion, 4K.

------------------------------------------------------------
3. SEALCOATING APPLICATION — SQUEEGEE PASS
------------------------------------------------------------
Side angle, knee height. A worker in a hi-vis shirt pulls a wide squeegee
through wet coal-black sealer, leaving a glossy band beside faded grey
asphalt. The wet edge catches an orange sunset reflection. Slow motion,
120fps conformed to 24fps, droplets visible. Industrial and meditative.

------------------------------------------------------------
4. LINE STRIPING — MACHINE PASS AT DUSK
------------------------------------------------------------
Tracking alongside a walk-behind striping machine laying a bright white line
across dark new asphalt at dusk. Fresh paint glistens. Traffic cones with
reflective bands blur in the foreground. Cool blue dusk sky contrasts the
warm work lights. Camera height 2 ft, parallel dolly move, seamless loop.

------------------------------------------------------------
5. CRACK FILLING — HOT RUBBER DETAIL
------------------------------------------------------------
Macro shot of hot rubberized crack filler flowing from an applicator wand
into a crack, slight steam, material self-leveling glossy black. Worker's
glove and boot soft in background. Single continuous 10-second pour.

------------------------------------------------------------
6. DRONE ORBIT — CREW AND PAVER
------------------------------------------------------------
70 ft altitude drone orbit (180 degrees) around a working asphalt paver and
three-person crew laying a residential driveway. Dump truck feeding the
hopper. Fresh black mat contrasts the tan graded base. Rural Ohio setting,
big sky, late-day light. Slow constant orbit speed for loop potential.

------------------------------------------------------------
