Service · Maintenance

Protect it early.
Repair it right.

Regular inspections, crack filling, sealcoating and striping help a parking lot last longer and cost less to own. We focus on the work your pavement needs now, before small problems become expensive ones.

Request Estimate Call Now

Pavement is affordable to protect early and expensive to rebuild after years of neglect. Routine maintenance costs far less than major repairs and helps keep the lot safe, clean and useful for customers.

We walk the lot, take clear photos and point out open cracks, standing water, faded markings and loose surfaces. Then we recommend the work the pavement actually needs, with no extras added just to grow the job.

You get clear priorities and real numbers for the work ahead. That makes budgeting easier, reduces surprises and helps the property owner make sound decisions before damage spreads.

Straight Answers

Parking Lot Maintenance FAQ

What does regular maintenance cost?

Cost depends on the lot's size and condition, but regular maintenance is usually a small fraction of one repaving project and can delay replacement for years.

We've deferred maintenance for years. Too late?

Usually not. We'll stabilize what can be saved, identify what is failing and handle the worst areas first.

Do you handle winter damage?

Yes. Spring inspections look for cracking from changing temperatures, plow scrapes and new potholes so damage can be corrected before it spreads.

Can this fit a fixed annual budget?

Yes. We work within your number and put structural protection ahead of cosmetic work.
