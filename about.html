About Hester

Sealcoating is the trade.
Reliability is the business.

Hester Asphalt Sealcoating & Line Striping LLC is Warren Hester's working crew out of Leesburg, Ohio — not a franchise, not a broker. The person who quotes your job answers for it.

Our Story

Built in Highland County, one driveway at a time.

Warren Hester has spent more than 30 years learning what makes asphalt work last. He started this company in 2006 by sealcoating driveways for neighbors in and around Leesburg and caring more about the quality of the work than the marketing of it. The work spoke for itself, referrals spread along US-50 through Hillsboro and Greenfield, and two decades later the standard remains the same: honest, reliable, quality driven asphalt protection.

That experience taught us that good work is only part of earning a reputation. Answering the phone, keeping appointments, explaining the estimate and standing behind the finished job matter too. We treat those responsibilities with the same care we bring to preparation, materials and clean edges.

That's the company you're hiring: a crew that takes the boring parts — returning calls, keeping appointments, honoring numbers — as seriously as the asphalt itself.

What We Stand On

Four things we bring to every job.

Workmanship

Thorough surface preparation, commercial grade sealer, hot rubberized crack filler and straight, clean lines. Attention to detail is what our reputation is built on.

Communication

Calls returned, appointments kept and questions answered. You will always know where your estimate and project stand.

Straight Pricing

The number on the estimate is the number on the invoice. Scope changes get discussed before they get built.

Respect for Property

Clean edges, protected landscaping, swept job sites. We leave properties better looking than we found them — that's the point.

Capabilities

Sized for driveways.
Equipped for lots.

We run our own sealcoating rig, hot crack filling kettle, striping machines and repair equipment. No subcontractors and no handoffs between trades. Warren and his crew handle your surface from preparation to the final stripe.

  • Residential: driveway sealcoating, crack filling and repairs.
  • Commercial: parking lot sealcoating, striping and maintenance for churches, schools, retail and offices.
  • HOA & community: shared drives and community lot maintenance.
  • Phased work: weekend scheduling and work completed one section at a time so businesses stay open.
